Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Q: What is hardware locking (HWID)?

Your license key is locked to your computer's unique hardware ID. This means the key will only work on the PC where you first activated it. This prevents key sharing and piracy.

Q: I got a new PC. How do I transfer my license?

Open a support ticket in our Discord server and request an HWID reset. You are allowed 1 free HWID reset per year. After that, additional resets may require purchasing a new license.

Q: Can I run multiple bots with one key?

No. Each license key is valid for one bot instance only. If you want to run 2 separate bots (for example, one for your gaming server and one for your community server), you'll need to purchase 2 keys.

Q: How do I get my license key after paying with card?

After payment, you'll be redirected to a thank you page with instructions. Join our Discord server, accept the rules, and create a ticket in the #support channel. Provide your Stripe order number and payment screenshot to receive your key (usually within 24 hours).

Q: What's the difference between Standard and Priority Support?

Standard Support (Monthly/Yearly): Get help via Discord tickets during normal hours.
Priority Support (Lifetime only): Your tickets are handled first, faster response times, and direct assistance with setup issues.

Q: Can I get a refund?

Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase if your license has not been activated. Once activated, refunds are not available. Monthly/yearly subscriptions can be cancelled anytime.

Q: Do I need coding knowledge to use this?

Basic setup requires some familiarity with running programs and following instructions. The Control Panel GUI makes configuration easy with no coding required. However, you'll need to set up a database and (optionally) a Lavalink server for music.

Q: Where does the bot run?

The bot runs on your own computer or server - you have full control. For 24/7 operation, you can host it on a VPS like Oracle Cloud (free tier available), Railway, or any other hosting service.
