Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

For the bot: Discord User IDs, message counts (not content), voice activity duration.

For purchases: Email address, payment info (via payment processor).

2. What We Don't Collect

We do not collect message content, voice audio, or personal information beyond Discord IDs.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Data is only shared with payment processors to complete transactions.

4. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data by contacting us or removing the bot from your server.
