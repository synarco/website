Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

For the Free Discord Bot:

  • Discord User IDs and Server IDs (for leveling, economy, settings)
  • Message counts (for XP, not message content)
  • Voice activity duration (for voice XP)

For Control Panel Purchases:

  • Email address (for license delivery)
  • Payment information (processed securely by payment provider)
  • Machine identifier (for license activation)

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide bot functionality
  • To process purchases and deliver licenses
  • To provide customer support

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Message content
  • Voice chat audio
  • Personal information beyond Discord IDs

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share with payment processors to complete transactions.

5. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data by contacting us or removing the bot from your server.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us via Discord.
