We use your information to:

3. Data Storage

Your license data is stored securely. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

5. Bot Data

The Evil Bot may collect and store:

This data is stored locally on your server or in your configured database. We do not have access to your bot's data.

6. Your Rights
